Banana Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 - 3
Ingredients Quantity
Bananas 1
Eggs 1
Milk 1 Cup
Vanilla Extract 1 Splash
Ground Oatmeal 1/2 Cup
Whole Flour 1/2 Cup
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Cinnamon 1 tbsp
Blueberries 1/2 Cup

Step 1: Mash banana, whisk in egg, milk, and vanilla.

Step 2: In a seperate bowl, mix Oatmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.

Step 3: Gently add liquid ingredients to dry, then add blueberries.

Step 4: Using 1/3 measuring cup, dole out mixture into frying pan - greased with coconut oil, heated to medium, reduced to low once browned.

Step 5: Cook until yummy, serve with crushed walnuts, fresh strawberries, and maple syrup.

French Toast

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 - 3
Ingredients Quantity
Thick Slice Bread 4 Slices
Eggs 5
Milk 1 Splash
Vanilla Extract 1 Splash
Ground CornFlakes 1 Cup
Cinnamon 1 tbsp

Step 1: Whisk egg, milk, and vanilla.

Step 2: Lay bread in flat casserole, pour mixture over bread, sprinkle cinnamon, flip bread and soak until saturated.

Step 3: Spread ground cornflakes on plate, quickly coat each side of bread and place in frying pan - greased with coconut oil, heated to medium, brown on each side until crisp.

Step 4: Remove from frying pan, place in oven - pre-heated to 350 fahrenheit.

Step 5: Cook until yummy, serve with fresh strawberries and maple syrup.

Toad in a Hole

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1/2
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 1 Slice
Egg 1
Salt 1 Shake
Pepper 2 Shakes

Step 1: Cut hole in middle of bread with cookie cutter or shot glass.

Step 2: Fry bread, including hole, in frying pan - greased with butter, heated to medium.

Step 3: Reduce heat and crack that egg! Add salt and pepper, flip and eat.
